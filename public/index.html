Welcome to CPI Productions' personalized birthday greetings to celebrate SPECIAL OCCASIONS. These mini-newspaper-style personalized greetings highlight what happened on the day you were born.

You can email or print these nostalgic greetings for many occasions, including BIRTHDAYS and ANNIVERSARIES, with the ability to add a personal message.

Professional salespeople use these greetings as a way to have a memorable contact with their clients to enhance client retention. An important feature allows you to send someone a greeting for a FUTURE date and to receive email REMINDERS for upcoming birthdays.

WHO WE ARE

CPI Productions LLC was founded by Courtney Bonacci. By day, she is a financial advisor at a large brokerage firm. She sends birthday emails to all of her clients but grew tired of emailing traditional birthday e-cards. She also wanted to illustrate to her clients the immense impact that time can have on your money and investments. Thus, www.bithdayheadliens.com and www.birthdaynewsletter.com was born.

The company, CPI Productions, stands for: C: Classic, Client, Communication, Connect, Contact, Customer, Customized P: Particular, Past, Personal, Personalized I: Important, In this year, Inflation, Information In essence, our goal is to help salespeople create automated meaningful, memorable interactions with their clients. We are happy to discuss custom solutions/templates for you and your company.
